Polaris North Star

Classic navigation symbol featuring the North Star (Polaris) with compass cardinal directions. The star represents finding your way home, while the prominent "N" emphasizes northern identity.

  • North Star symbolizes navigation & guidance
  • Cardinal compass points (N/S/E/W)
  • Glow rings suggest signal/beacon
  • Clean, symmetrical, professional
Best For:

Traditional, timeless appeal. Perfect for those who want classic navigation symbolism with northern emphasis.

2

GPS Location Beacon

Modern GPS pin with emergency signal rings and aurora accent. Directly represents the app's core functionality: tracking location and sending emergency signals.

  • GPS pin = location tracking (safety)
  • Signal rings = emergency beacon
  • Aurora waves = northern lights (north)
  • Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
Best For:

Tech-savvy users. Most literal representation of app features (GPS + emergency signal).

3

Professional Compass Rose

Traditional compass rose with all 8 cardinal/intercardinal points. The aurora-gradient north arrow and central beacon combine navigation with safety monitoring.

  • Full 8-point compass rose
  • Aurora-gradient north (emphasis)
  • Central beacon pulse (safety)
  • Most detailed, sophisticated option
Best For:

Serious outdoor professionals. Most comprehensive navigation symbolism with polished execution.

4

Aurora Northern Lights

Stunning aurora borealis waves with bold north arrow. Most visually distinctive option, emphasizing northern Canadian identity with natural beauty.

  • Flowing aurora waves (northern lights)
  • Bold directional arrow (north)
  • Safety beacon at base (emergency)
  • Most unique & memorable design
Best For:

Maximum northern identity and visual impact. Perfect for brand recognition and standing out.
